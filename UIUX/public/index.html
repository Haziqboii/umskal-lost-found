System Flow Process

1

Report lost or found item: File details with a photo upload.

2

Found item is handed to management: Finder must hand it physically to counter.

3

Management verifies the item: Admin physically reviews custody.

4

Owner submits claim with proof: Real owners identify item and submit proof details.

5

Management approves the claim: Admin reviews audits.

6

Owner collects item: Handover completed physically at counter.
